Brief Introduction to Android Netrunner

Andriod: Netrunner is an asymetrical card game for two players. One player plays as the corporation, or 'corp' for short. Their goal is to advance agendas and protect them with protective programs known as 'ice'.

The other player plays as a runner, which is where the game gets its name. Their goal is to infiltrate the corps defenses, steal their agendas, and foil their plans. The two sides have different cards to represent this, and the asymetrical nature of the game leads to exciting tactical battles.

Netrunner is a living card game, meaning new sets of cards are released semi-regularly. The constant influx of new cards, combines with the nuanced deckbuilding to provide a lot of opportunities for player expression. This, the exciting asymetric nature of the game and the rich worldbuilding that accompanies it, are just some of the reasons people all over the world enjoy playing Andriod: Netrunner. The game is currently managed by Null Signal Games, and you can visit their website to learn more.
